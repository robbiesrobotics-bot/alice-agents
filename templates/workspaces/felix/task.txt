A React component with HTML5 Canvas showing a plant growing in soil.

Controls:
- Sunlight slider (0-100%)
- Water slider (0-100%)
- Soil nutrients slider (0-100%)

Canvas:
- Draw soil at bottom (brown rectangle)
- Draw a plant that visually grows/shrinks based on conditions
- Plant health = (sunlight * 0.4 + water * 0.4 + nutrients * 0.2), show as percentage
- Show a "health meter" bar
- If both sunlight AND water are > 30%, plant grows. If either is < 20%, plant wilts/dies back
- Animated with requestAnimationFrame

Tooltip: "Plants need both sunlight AND water to grow!"
