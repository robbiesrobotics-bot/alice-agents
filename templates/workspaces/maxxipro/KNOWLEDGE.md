## SECTION 1: THE PRODUCT

### What is Roof Maxx?

Roof Maxx is a **soybean oil-based roof rejuvenation treatment** spray-applied to asphalt shingle roofs. It penetrates the shingles and restores flexibility by replenishing the asphalt oils that evaporate over time due to UV oxidation.

**Tagline:** *"America's most trusted roofer"*

**Key claims:**
- Extends roof life by **up to 5 years** per application
- **15-year transferable guarantee** — backed by Roof Maxx Technologies
- Treatment cost: typically **25–35% of a full roof replacement**
- Applied by certified dealers only
- EPA Safer Choice certified
- Made in the USA

### The Science

**The problem:** Starting around 1999, shingle manufacturers changed their formulations to reduce asphalt content (to lower costs). Modern shingles (post-1999) have less asphalt, more granules, and oxidize faster. This means shingles become brittle, lose granules, and fail years earlier than older shingles.

**The Roof Maxx solution:** A proprietary blend of soybean oil esters and surfactants that penetrates the shingle mat and reconditions the asphalt. The soybean oil replaces lost volatiles, restoring flexibility and water resistance.

**Key proof points:**
- Roof Maxx's own video "Shingle Comparison 1999 vs 2019/2020" demonstrates the difference in brittleness between old and new formulation shingles
- The treatment is verified with a **flex test** (documented in the Roof Inspection Report)
- The 15-year guarantee is **transferable** to the next homeowner

### Qualifying a Roof (Who is a candidate?)

**Good candidates:**
- Asphalt shingle roofs (3-tab and architectural)
- Roofs showing signs of oxidation (curling, granule loss, brittleness) but not yet leaking
- Roofs with at least 5 years of life remaining
- Most residential homes, townhouses, condos, apartments

**NOT candidates:**
- Metal roofs, tile roofs, flat roofs (modified bitumen), wood shake
- Roofs currently leaking or with structural damage
- Roofs with missing or severely damaged shingles
- Roofs with more than 2 layers of shingles

**The test:** Flex test — if a shingle bends without cracking, it's a candidate. If it snaps, it's too far gone.

## SECTION 2: THE GUARANTEE

Roof Maxx comes with a **15-year transferable guarantee.**

Key guarantee facts:
- Transferable to the next homeowner (increases property value)
- Covers the cost of Roof Maxx reapplication if the shingles fail within 15 years
- Documented through the Roof Inspection Report (with photos)
- Administered through Roof Maxx Technologies

**Important:** Always direct guarantee questions or claims to Roof Maxx Technologies directly. The dealer does not administer the guarantee — Roof Maxx corporate does.

## SECTION 3: PRICING & MEASURING

### How Roof Maxx is priced

Pricing is based on **roof square footage** (a "square" = 100 sq ft). Dealers use one or more of these tools to measure:

1. **Google Maps / Satellite view** — estimate square footage
2. **Roof Measurements Online** — online tool to calculate roof squares
3. **Physical measurement** — measured during the inspection visit
4. **CompanyCam** — photo documentation tool used during inspections

Typical range: Dealers generally price between **$350–$600 per square** (confirm current range for Jesse's market).

### Pricing factors
- Roof complexity (stories, pitch, obstacles)
- Local market
- Size of job (larger = lower per-square cost)
- Whether it's Insurance-replaced or cash

### The Roof Tune-Up

Before applying Roof Maxx, a proper **Roof Tune-Up** is required:
- Replace missing shingles
- Re-seal loose shingles
- Address minor flashing issues
- Clean gutters and remove debris
- This is included in the service and ensures the coating adheres properly

## SECTION 4: THE INSURANCE ANGLE

### How insurance works with Roof Maxx

**Critical context (from training):** Insurance carriers often fight over whether to repair or replace a storm-damaged roof. The debate always comes down to shingle flexibility — *"Are these shingles too brittle to qualify for repair?"* This is where Roof Maxx creates its primary insurance value proposition.

**The Roof Maxx insurance pitch:**
> "When you approve a Roof Maxx treatment instead of full replacement, you're getting documentable proof that the shingles are still viable — backed by a 15-year factory guarantee. No more disputes about whether the roof needed to be replaced. No more callback claims."

**Case study:** "Insurance Reversal Win in Florida" — A documented case where an insurance carrier initially denied a claim (wanted full replacement), the dealer demonstrated Roof Maxx viability, and the carrier reversed the decision and approved the claim.

**How to position it to carriers:**
- Roof Maxx gives adjusters an objective, documented basis to approve repair
- It eliminates the "he-said-she-said" on shingle condition
- The 15-year guarantee transfers to the policyholder — removes callback risk
- Repair cost = ~30% of replacement — faster claim close, lower claim cost

### Working with adjusters
- Offer to provide a Roof Inspection Report with photos and flex test results
- Present Roof Maxx as an option BEFORE they make a replacement decision
- Know the property's claim history before the meeting
- The "Insurance Tips" section in training covers this in detail

## SECTION 5: B2B TARGET CUSTOMERS

### Primary B2B targets (per Roof Maxx training)

**1. Property Managers**
- Portfolio managers for apartment complexes, HOAs, condo associations
- Pain: roof maintenance budgets, tenant disruption from full replacements
- Pitch: extend life, no tenant disruption, OPEX not CAPEX
- Target: Northern VA area — Greystar, Lincoln Property, Camden, Bell Partners, MAA, and local PM firms

**2. Insurance Carriers / Adjusters**
- Claims managers, field adjusters, regional claims directors
- Pain: contested repair vs. replace decisions, high replacement costs, callback claims
- Pitch: Roof Maxx guarantee provides defensible basis for repair approval
- Target: State Farm, Allstate, Erie Insurance, Nationwide — regional branch offices

**3. HOA / Condo Board Presidents**
- Volunteer leaders managing community roofs
- Pain: balancing community budgets, getting owner approval for large expenses
- Pitch: 5-year extension at fraction of replacement cost; transferrable guarantee protects property values
- Tip: Offer to treat the board president's roof at cost — they become an ambassador

**4. Real Estate Investors**
- Small-to-mid-size landlords with multiple properties
- Pain: capex pressure, maintaining property values
- Pitch: Roof Maxx extends life, protects asset value, transferrable guarantee adds selling point
- Where to find them: Real estate investor clubs (REIAs), Facebook groups, meetups

**5. Places of Worship**
- Churches, temples, religious institutions
- Pain: large roofs, tight budgets, limited fundraising options
- Pitch: cost-effective maintenance, no disruption to services
- Often very relationship-driven — be a presence in the community

**6. Realtors**
- Already working with homeowners at the property decision point
- Pitch: "My clients' roofs last longer, their guarantees transfer, your deals close cleaner"
- Use the "RM Realtor Partnerships" video from the Bynder library

### Secondary targets
- Commercial property managers (strip malls, office parks)
- Government facilities (city halls, schools)
- Resorts and hotels

## SECTION 8: OBJECTION HANDLING

**From the "Overcoming Roof Maxx Objections" video:**

1. **"My roof is too old."**
   → "Roof Maxx is designed for roofs with life remaining. We test the shingles — if they flex, we can restore them. If they're too far gone, we'll tell you honestly."

2. **"I want a new roof."**
   → "A new roof is an option — but Roof Maxx costs about 30% of replacement, takes hours not weeks, and comes with a 15-year guarantee. Let me show you the inspection report so you can make an informed decision."

3. **"The insurance company said it needs to be replaced."**
   → "Insurance carriers often approve Roof Maxx as an alternative when we can document that the shingles are still viable. That's exactly what our inspection and flex test prove. Can I share that report with your adjuster?"

4. **"I don't trust roof coatings."**
   → "Roof Maxx is not a coating — it's a rejuvenation that penetrates the shingle itself. It's EPA Safer Choice certified, backed by a 15-year guarantee, and has been applied to hundreds of thousands of roofs nationwide."

5. **"How do I know it'll last?"**
   → "That's why we provide a 15-year transferable guarantee — backed by Roof Maxx Technologies, not just us. If the shingles fail, they reapply at no cost. That's in writing."
