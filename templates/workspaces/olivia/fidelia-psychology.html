About

A practice built on
knowing the whole child.

We believe every child makes sense — when you take the time to really listen.

We work with children and adolescents navigating anxiety, attention challenges, school stress, and the everyday weight of growing up. Our approach is gentle and research-informed, guided by nine values that shape every session. We meet each child where they are — not where we expect them to be.

Our Foundation

Nine ways we show up for you.

These are the values that guide every session, every conversation, every relationship we build.

Love
Amor
Every child who walks through our door is a person worth knowing, not a problem to fix.
Joy
Alegría
We notice the small wins and celebrate them. Even the hard work can hold moments of lightness.
Peace
Paz
Our sessions are slow, quiet, and free from pressure. A calm place in a busy world.
Patience
Paciencia
We don't rush healing. We hold the pace each child needs, for as long as it takes.
Kindness
Bondad
Warmth isn't something we turn on for sessions. It's just how we are with your family, always.
Goodness
El Bien
We're honest about what we know and what we don't. Your child's wellbeing is the only guide.
Faithfulness
Fidelidad
We show up — through the setbacks, the slow climbs, the hard weeks. Every time.
Gentleness
Ternura
Hard things get talked about here. We hold those moments carefully, and never push harder than a child is ready for.
Self-Control
Templanza
We help children build steadiness from the inside out — skills they'll carry long after our work together is done.
