## Description

Most AI tools give you one assistant. A.L.I.C.E. gives you a whole team.

Run `npx @robbiesrobotics/alice-agents` and in under 60 seconds you have 28 AI specialists ready to work — a marketer, engineer, researcher, finance lead, security analyst, product strategist, and more. Coordinated by Olivia, your AI team lead.

No account required to start. No API configuration maze. No prompt engineering. Just install and ask.

**How it works:**
Tell Olivia what you're working on. She routes your request to the right specialists, who collaborate and deliver structured, actionable output — not a wall of hallucinated text.

**Three tiers:**
- **Starter (Free)** — 10 agents, zero sign-up required
- **Pro ($49 one-time)** — All 28 agents + persistent memory, pay once own forever
- **Cloud Add-on ($20/mo)** — Hosted Mission Control dashboard, requires Pro

A.L.I.C.E. works where you work: CLI, Telegram, or iMessage. Persistent memory means your team remembers your projects, preferences, and history.

I built this because I was tired of stitching together AI tools that don't talk to each other. A.L.I.C.E. is what I wanted to exist. Now it does.

Try the free tier. Break it. Tell me what you think.
