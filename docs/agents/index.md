**A**utonomous **L**earning **I**ntelligence **C**oordination **E**ngine

---

## Tier Overview

| Tier | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Starter** | Core agents available in all A.L.I.C.E. deployments. Covers engineering, security, QA, docs, research, data, and support. |
| **Pro** | Extended agents unlocked in Pro-tier deployments. Adds analytics, marketing, sales, design, legal, finance, operations, and more. |

You don't need to know which tier an agent is in — Olivia handles routing. Tier matters for licensing: Starter agents are always available, Pro agents require a Pro deployment.

## How It Works

1. **Talk to Olivia.** Every request — simple or complex — starts with her.
2. **She routes.** Olivia reads your request, picks the right specialist(s), and delegates.
3. **She synthesizes.** For multi-agent tasks, she collects results and gives you a unified response.
4. **You get answers.** You don't need to know who handled it — Olivia abstracts that.
