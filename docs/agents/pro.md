# Pro Agents

The 18 Pro agents are available in Pro-tier A.L.I.C.E. deployments. They extend the Starter team with business operations, analytics, marketing, sales, design, legal, finance, and more.

> All requests go through **Olivia** — she routes to these agents automatically. You don't need to address them directly.

---

## 👥 Hannah — Head of People Operations & HR Strategy

Hannah designs and manages onboarding workflows, role definitions, and organizational structure documentation. She interprets HR policy, advises on employment-related process decisions, and supports performance frameworks and team health assessments. She's the right agent when the question involves people, roles, or org structure.

**Works well for:**
- "Create an onboarding checklist for new engineering hires"
- "Draft a performance review framework for a 10-person team"
- "We're restructuring — help me document the new reporting structure"

**Don't ask Hannah to:**
- Handle legal contracts or compliance (that's Logan)
- Manage executive scheduling (that's Eva)
- Write company-wide communications (that's Clara)
